Honey Butter Country Fare
⭐ 4.8
Farm-to-table brunch · North Portland
"Incredible farm-fresh food with a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Everything on the menu is made with love and top-quality ingredients."
📍 6550 N Interstate Ave ⚠ Saturday 12–6pm only · CLOSED Sun & Mon
Menu highlights
Seasonal egg plate farm-fresh eggs, rotating veg, house toast must try
Biscuits & honey butter house-made, the reason for the name
Morning grain bowl hearty, vegetable-forward, crowd favorite
La Perlita
⭐ 4.7
Mexican breakfast & coffee · North Portland
"Authentic Mexican food in a cozy spot. The breakfast burritos are loaded and satisfying — great coffee too."
📍 5610 N Interstate Ave 🕐 Daily 8am–4pm
Menu highlights
Breakfast burrito chorizo, egg, potato, cheese — stuffed must try
Huevos rancheros house salsa, refried beans, tortillas
Café de olla traditional Mexican spiced coffee
Berlu Bakery
⭐ 4.7
Vietnamese-inspired fusion bakery · SE Belmont
"Creative desserts with Vietnamese-inspired names — but not authentic Vietnamese pastries. Go in knowing that and you'll love it." — Vy Nguyen
📍 661 SE Belmont St ⚠ Thu–Sun 10am–3pm · Thu also 5–8pm · CLOSED Mon–Wed
Menu highlights
Mango roll cake repeatedly called out by regulars — get this order this
Pistachio/Pandan jasmine tea latte the drink pairing — don't skip it
Roasted coconut water go-to with the sweets, mentioned by multiple regulars

Seasonal pickle rotating, always worth getting — ask what's on

Khao Moo Dang
⭐ 4.6
Thai · SE Hawthorne
"I have never had ginger incorporated as a flavor in a broth — mixed with the coconut milk it created such a unique taste. They really mean it when they say pork belly is crispy." — John Smith
📍 3145 SE Hawthorne Blvd 🕐 Daily 11am–9pm (Fri–Sat til 9:30pm)
Menu highlights
Ba-Mhee Tom-Kha (#12) ginger-coconut broth, noodles, crispy pork belly — the one order this
Curry noodles (#8) bold, spicier than expected — flagged by multiple regulars
Cha Ma-Nao Thai limeade — the drink to order
Nong's Khao Man Gai
⭐ 4.6
Thai · SE Ankeny
"White meat was a bit dry, dark meat tasted much better. The pork knuckle was very tender and well braised, and the rice was soaked with the sauce — really flavorful." — Z Z
📍 609 SE Ankeny St 🕐 Daily 9am–9pm
Menu highlights
Pork knuckle rice (#4) well-braised, rice soaked in sauce — edges out the chicken order this
Khao man gai (#1) get dark meat, not white — and load the fermented sauce
Bitter melon soup + fermented sauce off-menu knowledge — ask for it, worth it
